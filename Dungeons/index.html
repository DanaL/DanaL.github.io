D&D Stuff

This is a place for me to post info for the current D&D campaign I'm running (which I tend to nickname - for obtuse reasons - megaverbrew) so the players can easily find it. The campaign is set in a homebrewed world, but hews fairly close to 5e D&D's rules and default setting. The world in my mind has a deep and rich history and plenty of mysterious secrets. I love that stuff and really have to resist the urge to lore dump on my poor players...
